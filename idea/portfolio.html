peer-to-peer messaging, Flutter / Android

A peer-to-peer messaging app that routes over Bluetooth and Wi-Fi transport layers instead of relying on a central server. Includes a hardened WebSocket chat service (singleton lifecycle, stream controller disposal, type-cast fixes) and a manifest hardened for BLE/Wi-Fi mesh networking — TLS enforcement, explicit service exports, data extraction rules.
